Visiting

Visiting Satang Jabang is possible. An increasing number of tourists visits the school.

The school Satang Jabang is located in the place Kafountine, in the department of Bignona in Senegal.

Why visit Satang Jabang?

When you visit Satang Jabang, you can learn about Senegalese culture and second chance education.

Accessibility

You can reach Satang Jabang from the N5 to Diouloulou. You then have to follow a road to Bandajikaki. Keep following the road until you have reached Kafountine.

Satang Jabang is located between the Mini Marché and Credit Mutuel du Sénégal, at the main road of Kafountine.

Accomodations

The availability of electricity is limited in Kafountine and there is no flowing drinking water.

The school has a restaurent. The food there has a safety is reasonable integrity.

Close to Satang Jabang is a bank, a small grocery store, two mosques, and further away is a petrol station.
